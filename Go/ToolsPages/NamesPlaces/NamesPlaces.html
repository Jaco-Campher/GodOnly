Names and Places

Sometimes one place or person can have more than one name, for instance Abram became Abraham.

Abraham is not on the list as his name has no end times prophetic implications that I’m aware of, but you get the idea.

Places or nations that have been identified will also be added to the list. When reading Scripture on this site, the words from the Names / Places will be displayed when matches are found to hopefully help us better understand prophesy.
