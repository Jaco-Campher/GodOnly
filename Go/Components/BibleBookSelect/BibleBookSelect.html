Other Books

To better understand, it is recommended to read the complete book of 2 Esdras. But the short version is that after the Temple was destroyed with Jerusalem, all the books in the Temple were destroyed as well. In the verses below, Ezra is asking that God sends the Holy Spirit so that the books might be preserved.

If you want to know more about some of these books, the Cepher Publishing Group has a very good PDF on their website that gives some more details. It starts on page five if you want to skip the names section.

In some of these books there are one or two verses that can seemingly contradict the Bible, but then everything else fits perfectly. Keep in mind that these books are not meant for everyone, and one way to keep them out of the Bible would be to include one or two "mistakes".
