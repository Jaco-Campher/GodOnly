About

The purpose of this site is to provide study tools and studies to help better understand prophesies, and to live according to the Word of God and not the ways of the world.

All doctrines and believes must come from the Word of God. Scripture should be the sole authority on what is right and wrong.

Even though I am not a King James (KJV) only believer, I do prefer the King James version of the bible for the following reasons:

  • It is old enough to not to be influenced by today’s corruption. I am sure there was corruption when the KJV was translated as well, but hopefully less than in our modern world.
  • It has the Strong and Lexicon data readily available.
  • It is in the public domain. (Except in England)

But I do not believe that there is a single perfect translation of scripture, KJV or otherwise.


The bible used on this site is the KJV 1769 version. I did not specifically choose this version, it just checked all the requirement that was needed:

  • It is available in the USFM file format.
  • It includes the Apocrypha.
  • It includes Strong and Lexicon data.
