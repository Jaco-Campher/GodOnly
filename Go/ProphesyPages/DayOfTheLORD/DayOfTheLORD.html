Day of Salvation?

Most people believe that the Day of the Yah refers to the rapture or day of salvation. But looking at some of the verses below, we don’t see light and salvation, instead we see something entirely different.


This is describing a day of wrath and in Zaphaniah we see a very detailed description of this.
