Revelation 12 Sign

What is the Revelation 12 Sign?

It is a sign in the stars that is described in Revelation 12:1-2 that was fulfilled on 23 September 2017.


The woman clothed with the sun is the constellation of Virgo.

The crown of 12 stars is represented by the constellation of Leo (9 Stars) with Mercury, Mars and Venus making up the remaining 3 “stars” or wandering stars if you will.

Jupiter represents the child, staying in the womb of Virgo for 9 months.
