Timeline Dates Explanation

Warning

The dates in the timeline as they are shown now, is based on a few assumptions. So please do not take them too seriously. They are simply added in an attempt to better understand Revelation, specifically where we are in the timeline and how long it will be.

Calculation

If you look at Trumpet 1, you will see the 1260 days of the 2 witnesses starts at the beginning of Trumpet 1, and then continues until the end of Trumpet 6 (not shown yet). If we divide the 1260 days by the 6 trumpets, we get 210 days per trumpet, or about 7 months. I do not believe we can use this to show the exact start dates of the trumpets, but it should give us an estimated start month and duration for each, give or take a month.

1st Assumption

If the trumpets are 7 months long, then is it not possible that the seals and vials are also 7 months long? There is a reference in the Ascension of Isaiah that talks about another 1317 days period.


I have not found the exact location were this will fit into the timeline, so it has not been added here yet, but I do believe it is after the ascension of the 2 witnesses. 1317 days is about 2 months longer than the 1260 days, but it is in the same general ballpark as the 1260 days. If this is indeed after the 1260 days, that would indicate another 3.5+ years for the vials.

If we assume that all 3 groups (Seals, Trumpets, Vials) are equal, this will give us about 10.5 years if we count the first 6 of each. If we then assume that Seal 7, Trumpet 7, and Vial 7 are also around 7 months each we get a total time of about 12 years.

2nd Assumption

I have not found additional information on Seal 1, at least not enough to be able to say that it is going to happen on earth in the physical realm, so we will have to look at the other Seals to get a starting point.

If you look at global events today, there seems to be chaos everywhere. Now this is nothing new, but is does feel like it got a lot worse after the Israel – Gaza war started in 2023.

Seal 2 is "to take peace from the earth, and that they should kill one another". The current assumption for the dates in the timeline is that the war in Israel is the starting point of Seal 2 or at least that Oct 2023 is.

This alone is not a very conclusive point to peg the timeline to, but if we see the other Seals happen in in their respective timeslots, then we will have a better idea of the actual timeline.

Biblical Years

If we work backwards from the start of Seal 2 and subtract 7 months, we end up in Mar 2023. This is the same month as the beginning of the biblical new year.

I know this date can vary depending in what calendar you are using, but as we are only looking for a rough estimate and not to the day specific, this should be within about a month or so of most calendars.

Here we see the number 12 come up again. If the tribulation is indeed 12 years long, then each of the 12 parts will represent something that happens in that year, and we can see this with Seal 2.


Seal 2 starts 7 months after the biblical new year of 2023 and so is still part of the first year or first part shown in 2 Baruch.

Clicking on the year in the Revelation Timeline will show the verse for that year in 2 Baruch and any additional information that has been found.

Additional Notes

The positioning of the dates is only correct when the tabs are closed. The dates for each tab will not move when the tab is open as there is no direct correlation between the dates and the verses inside of each tab.

The verses in the tabs can show a lot of details for something that happens in a month but then only show a verse or 2 for something that happens over the next 5 or 6 months. Remember, these are general estimates only.
