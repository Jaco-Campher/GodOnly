We know that here will still be children born in the 1000 year reign, so there will still be some form of marriage.


Thing about what is going to happen at the end of the 1000 years of peace or even right after the rapture. Let’s say two people are married, if there is no marriage beyond either of these points, there is only two outcomes that I can think of:

  1. Everyone that is married then just stops loving each other. Sort of like saying: thanks, it was fun, but I don't want you anymore.
  2. People will still love each other and then Yah says, sorry, you are not allowed to love each other anymore and then separates them.

If you have spent any time reading the Bible you will agree that both scenarios does not fit what we find in the Bible, especially considering we are commanded to love each other and that Yah is love.

But assuming that this is the case, it shows a clear difference from when Eve was "brought" to Adam.
