Introduction

When listening to a study or sermon on a topic that you have not done your own study on, it is easy for it to make sense. By isolating a verse or even a section of a verse, you can make scripture say or mean anything. You can listen to a study now and agree with what is says. Then 6 weeks later you hear another teaching on the same topic and it can also make sense, even though it teaches something different.

The only way to truly know what scripture teaches on any topic, is to do your own studies.
